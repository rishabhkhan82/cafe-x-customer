How Cafe‑X POS works

A clear path from guests scanning your menu to serving and billing—without friction.

Step 1

Scan & browse

Guests scan the table QR to view your live menu with photos, dietary tags, and prices.

Instant updates from the owner panel—no page refresh.
Step 2

Place orders

Customers add items, quantities, and notes. Orders appear instantly on the KDS and owner dashboard.

Suggested add‑ons and combos increase average ticket size.
Step 3

Prepare & serve

Kitchen moves orders from Preparing → Ready. Waiters update On the way → Delivered.

Timers and color‑codes keep the pass efficient at peak.
Step 4

Bill & pay

Bill by table at the counter or tableside. Accept UPI, cards, or cash—offers and taxes applied automatically.

Reconciled reports are ready the moment payment is done.

Designed to be clear and calm—so your team moves faster with fewer taps.
